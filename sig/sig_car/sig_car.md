## SIG group work objectives and scope

### Work Objectives
- Enhance OpenHarmony's system basic capabilities/basic software services, complement Car proprietary software and hardware service framework capabilities, enhance API support for Car application development, and improve Car HDF proprietary driver support for cockpit hardware developer development in response to the demand for smart car.
- Provide corresponding IDE tool chain simulator/development board technology to support ecological partners to facilitate the development of Car applications and smart cockpit products, and prosper the OpenHarmony ecosystem.

### Work Scope
- Building the basic capabilities of OpenHarmony For Car's OS platform, mainly including the Car proprietary business subsystem and Car proprietary hardware subsystem;
- OpenHarmony For Car’s application service ecological technical support;
- OpenHarmony For Car’s smart cockpit hardware ecological technical support;
